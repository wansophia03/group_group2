about us

Greetings from Douche’ Cafe!

Our cafe name 'douche' means sweet in france. The owner of this cafe wants to keep the theme sweet and friendly. We started operating in 2019. Since then Douche 'Cafe has done its best to further expand its plans to open several more outlets in West Malaysia the next few years to come.

We offer great western food with local delights, served in the comfortable surrounding of our cafe. We believe that great food and friendly service can be offered for a reasonable price and therefore affordable for everyone to enjoy!!
